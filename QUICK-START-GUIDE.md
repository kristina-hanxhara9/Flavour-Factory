## 📱 **Step 1: Open the System** (1 minute)

### **Windows:**
1. Double-click `enhanced-setup.bat`
2. Choose option **2** (Enterprise Version)
3. Browser will open automatically

### **Mac/Linux:**
1. Double-click `enhanced-setup.sh` 
2. Choose option **2** (Enterprise Version)
3. Browser will open automatically

### **Manual:**
1. Double-click `enhanced-index.html`
2. Allow camera permissions when asked

## 🎛️ **Step 2: Import Demo Data** (1 minute)

1. **Click the ⚙️ Settings tab** at the top
2. **Scroll down to Data Management section**
3. **Click "📥 Importo të Dhënat"**

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting**

### **❌ Camera won't open?**
- **Chrome:** Go to Settings → Privacy → Camera → Allow
- **Safari:** Go to Preferences → Websites → Camera → Allow
- **Try different browser** (Chrome recommended)

### **❌ AI not recognizing products?**
- **Ensure good lighting**
- **Hold camera steady**
- **Try different angles**
- **Lower confidence threshold in Settings**

### **❌ App running slowly?**
- **Close other browser tabs**
- **Restart browser**
- **Check internet connection**
- **Try Chrome browser**

### **❌ Need help?**
- **Run:** `verify-system.sh` (Mac/Linux) or `verify-system.bat` (Windows)
- **Check:** `TROUBLESHOOTING-GUIDE.md`
- **Read:** `ENTERPRISE-README.md` for complete guide

## 💡 **Pro Tips for Success**

### **📸 Best Training Practices:**
- **15-20 photos minimum** per product
- **Different angles:** top, side, close-up, far away
- **Good lighting:** natural light or bright LED
- **Clear focus:** no blurry images
- **Various orientations:** rotate product

### **🚀 Performance Optimization:**
- **Use Chrome or Safari** (best AI performance)
- **Good internet** for initial AI model loading
- **8GB+ RAM** for large product catalogs
- **SSD storage** for faster loading

### **💾 Data Protection:**
- **Set up cloud backup immediately**
- **Export data weekly** as backup
- **Test restore procedures** regularly
- **Keep training photos** separately
